# Steak

![finished](IMG_1357_2.JPEG)

## Ingredients

- 10oz (0.62lb) of steak at room temp
- Oil (higher smoke point the better), used extra light. Grapeseed or canola oil
- 2 cloves of garlic (cut into halfs)
- Fresh thyme
- 1 tablespoon of butter, room temp
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Recipe

### Prep

- Take steaks out of fridge for 20min
- Cut excessive fat around edges as needed
- Dry the steaks with papers towels
- Preheat a large stainless steel pan with oil at medium-high heat
- Season one side with salt, then immediately go to sear

### Sear

- Put seasoned side into pan to sear for ~2-3min
- Salt unseasoned side
- Flip steak and sear
- Sear all sides with prongs for about 30s each side
- Turn heat to low

### Baste

- Add butter, garlic, and thyme and baste steaks
- Rub cut side of garlic on top of steak

### Rest

- Take stake out and rest for 10min
- Add pepper
