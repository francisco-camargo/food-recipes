# Instant Rice

## Ingredients

- 1 cup instant white rice (or jasmine rice)
- 1.5 cups water
- 1 tbsp light olive oil
- 1 tsp salt
- 0.5 tsp garlic powder

## Recipe

- Add rice to the Aroma rice cooker inner pot
- Add water to the pot
- Add olive oil, salt, and garlic powder
- Stir gently to distribute ingredients
- Place inner pot into rice cooker and close lid
- Turn the pressure release knob to "Sealing" position
- Press the "Rice" button (typically cooks for 12-15 minutes)
- When cooking is complete, allow natural pressure release for 5 minutes
- Carefully turn knob to "Venting" to release remaining pressure
- Open lid and fluff rice with a fork
- Serve immediately

## Tips

- Do not open the lid during cooking!
- For softer rice, add an extra 1/4 cup of water
- Let rice rest for a few minutes after cooking for better texture
- Clean the pressure valve regularly for consistent results
